Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Summize! Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is respected and protected in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Information We Do Not Collect

At Summize, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. This means we do not gather any identifiable information such as your name, email address, or any other details that could be used to identify you. We believe in maintaining your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains confidential.

Data Usage and Handling

Summize primarily functions to summarize texts or URLs provided by users. When you input text or a link, we process it solely to generate summaries. Importantly, we do not store or retain any text or URL data on our servers. Once the summarization is complete, the data is not saved, ensuring that your information remains private and secure.

AdMob and Third-Party Advertisements

Our app utilizes AdMob, a service provided by Google, to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information from your device, including your advertising ID, to provide you with relevant ads. This information helps us generate revenue to keep the app free for users while ensuring a personalized advertising experience.

For more details on how AdMob collects and uses your information, we encourage you to review the AdMob Privacy Policy. Please note that we do not have control over the data collection practices of AdMob or any other third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Summize is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we will revise the effective date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy or the practices of our application, please feel free to reach out to us at development@ciiplo.com. We value your feedback and are here to assist you.
