Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Summize. This privacy policy describes how we handle your information when you use our application.

Personal Data

Summize does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We respect your privacy and ensure that your personal information remains confidential.

Data Handling

Summize primarily functions to summarize texts or URLs provided by users. We do not store or retain any text or URL data on our servers.
